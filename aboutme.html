Who am I?

I’ve spent over eight years in IT, working mainly as an Oracle Utilities Application Administrator across systems like CC&B, OUCSS, OUMDM, WebLogic, and Oracle Database 12c.

After moving to New Zealand and taking time off to raise my family, I decided to pivot and explore something I’ve always been curious about — data. That led me to a Data Analyst internship with Industry Connect and MVP Studio.

Since then, I’ve been working with tools like SQL Server, Power BI, SSIS, SSRS, Tableau, and Python — building dashboards, cleaning and transforming data, and digging into the numbers to find stories and insights.

I enjoy solving problems, learning new tools, and turning messy data into something meaningful and useful.

What I’m Doing

  • Data Analysis

    Transforming raw data into actionable insights using SQL, Power BI, and SSRS for dynamic visualizations and reporting.

  • Data Engineering

    Building efficient ETL pipelines using SSIS to manage data flow into warehouses.

  • Business Intelligence

    Designing interactive dashboards and reports with Power BI and Tableau, enhancing decision-making with KPIs and drill-through features.

  • Systems Support

    Managing Oracle applications and WebLogic systems ensuring infrastructure and application stability.
